Summary

Blood, Sweat and Charity is the first and only guide to address the needs of participants in charity challenges of every kind, from an hour of running to a month of cycling. With the help of hundreds of experts it guides you through the range of choices, the fundraising, the training and the preparation, as well as inspiring readers with examples and profiles of different challenges and participants.
