Charities

After a successful first year in the UK and abroad, Eye Books has pledged to donate £5 to your charity for every copy of Blood, Sweat and Charity – The Charity Challenge Handbook bought by your supporters.

1000s of challenge participants have benefited from using Blood, Sweat and Charity to raise more money and take on more challenges, and now Eye Books wants to thank the charities whose supporters have bought the book.

10,000 books are available now.

Want to let your challenge participants and supporters know about the offer?

E-mail nick@eye-books to request some free flyers that can be included in any mail outs or registration packs. Or if you want to include news of the offer in any of your own materials, click here to copy and paste some suggested text.

When your supporters buy the book online, they will be able to choose their favourite charity to benefit from the donation. Every month, we will update you on how many have been sold and send through the donation.

The more supporters you let know about the offer, the bigger the check at the end of every month!

So what can Blood, Sweat and Charity do for your supporters?

Find out what the first and only charity challenge handbook has done for participants in every kind of challenge and around the world.
