THE CHARITY CHALLENGE HANDBOOK

by Nick Stanhope

“Distils the experience of hundreds of experts and participants.”
Independent on Sunday

"Finally a practical, positive guide for anyone thinking of taking on a charity challenge...A best practice handbook.”
Maria Photiou, Challenge Events Manager, ActionAid

"It will transform the way our supporters fundraise and train."
Dr Mike Meegan, International Director, ICROSS

Blood, Sweat and Charity is the first and only guide to address the needs of charity challenge participants. Whatever your age, fitness or motivations, this guide will give you exactly what you need to achieve that remarkable feat, hit that fundraising target and make a lasting difference to yourself and the world.

Drawing upon the knowledge of 100s of experts in each field, the handbook provides clear guidance to help participants overcome common stumbling blocks: how to be a successful fundraiser, how to train safely and efficiently, how to make the right choices and how to plan and execute independent challenges. Alongside this advice, case studies and profiles of previous challenges will inspire and inform you.
